1. **Click on the <img src="/images/hunarho_small_logo.png" style={{ width: '40px', height: '40px', display: 'inline', }} /> icon** on the address bar to open up the recording popup.

2. If this is your first time opening, you might be prompted with camera and microphone permissions. Click **Allow** to give Screenity permission to record your microphone and camera.

3. If you'd like to show your camera in your recording, **select your device on the camera dropdown**.
   - Once it‚Äôs enabled, you will be able to flip the camera, and enable custom camera backgrounds.

4. **Set the microphone** through the dropdown below.
   - If you want, you can enable **Push to Talk** to only record while the `Alt+Shift+U` keys are pressed (`Option+Shift+U` on Mac).

   > ‚ùì You can check if your microphone is picking up sound by looking at the green audio waveform.

5. You can set any additional settings by clicking on **Show more options**.

6. Click the **Start recording** button to start making your video.
